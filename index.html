Abstract

The chess game is a long game full of details. The project goal is to summarize a chess game to its algebraic notation for players who want to learn many methods, analyze their games quickly and efficiently. We also offer a visualization of the chessboard throughout the game. Our method can read the board and detect where the pieces are on the board. Given the board, the piece's location, and the game's order, we can determine their algebraic notation. And to present a visualization of the board at any given point of the game.
